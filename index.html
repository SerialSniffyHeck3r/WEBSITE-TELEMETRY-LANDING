IKARUS VARIO

GPS varios with built-in screens are often expensive, while simple entry-level varios can feel limited. IKARUS VARIO is designed as an accessible GPS vario with XCSoar connectivity, voice guidance, and a dedicated sunlight-readable display.

  • High-rate GPS logging
  • Pressure-based vario sensing
  • XCSoar BLE connectivity
  • Physical buttons for in-flight use
  • Waypoint and basic navigation display

For motorcycle riders

Motorcycle Telemetry

Many affordable telemetry devices are track-focused or rely heavily on a phone screen. This project is a standalone motorcycle GPS computer for road rides, touring, and occasional track days.

  • GPS ride logging
  • Lean angle and ride dynamics
  • Dedicated 3.8-inch display
  • Built-in battery, USB power, and vehicle power modes
  • Maintenance log and camera remote support planned

Why this exists

Not a phone mount. Not a track-only box.

Flight

A middle-ground flight instrument between simple entry-level varios and expensive full-featured flight computers.

Motorcycle

A dedicated ride computer for riders who want data on the device itself, not only inside a phone app.

Shared platform

Common hardware, different firmware targets: sunlight display, buttons, BLE, GPS logging, and field-ready power.
